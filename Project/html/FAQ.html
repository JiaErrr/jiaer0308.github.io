  • FAQ
  1. What is WOOF all about?
    • Woof is a unique clothing shop that combines fashion with a natural and dog-friendly lifestyle. Our collections are inspired by nature, and we celebrate the bond between humans and their canine companions.
  2. Are your products sustainable?
    • Yes, we are committed to sustainability. Our clothing is made from eco-friendly materials, and we strive to minimize our environmental impact throughout the production process.
  3. Do you have a size guide for your clothing?
    • Yes, we provide a detailed size guide on our website to help you choose the perfect fit. If you have any specific questions about sizing, feel free to reach out to our customer support.
  4. Can I return or exchange items if they don't fit?
    • Absolutely. We understand that sizing can be tricky sometimes. Check our Returns and Exchanges policy for more information on the process and conditions.
  5. How do I track my order?
    • Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the status of your order on the shipping carrier's site.
  6. Are there any promotions or discounts available?
    • We occasionally run promotions and discounts. Keep an eye on our website, subscribe to our newsletter, or follow us on social media to stay updated on the latest offers.
  7. Can I find Woof products in physical stores?
    • Currently, Woof is exclusively available online. We don't have physical stores, but our online platform provides a convenient and secure way to shop our collections.
  8. How do I contact customer support?
    • You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns.
  9. Do you ship internationally?
    • Yes, we offer international shipping. You can select your country during the checkout process, and shipping costs will be calculated accordingly.
